Top Anomalies

Today only. Which categories (Invoices, Vendors, …) are far from the 30-day baseline?

Drives Overall Risk & Management Warnings.

Multi-Day Pattern Alert

~5 days. Slow-burn: small changes that add up. Runs every fraud check once active.

Can be HIGH when today looks quiet.

Credit Card Watch

Today only. Unusual company card activity.

Independent of churn rows.

Do not skip Multi-Day when Top Anomalies looks fine. Overall Risk may stay LOW while Multi-Day shows HIGH — check both.

Daily checklist (Phase II)

  1. Run Today's Fraud Check — wait for finished.
  2. Overall Risk + Management Warnings → table above.
  3. Top Anomalies → note categories.
  4. Multi-Day Pattern Alert → note if not Clear.
  5. Credit Card Watch → note flags.
  6. Follow-up → QuickBooks Audit Log (who changed what).
