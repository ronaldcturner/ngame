NGAME College Lab — QuickBooks Sandbox Access

Instructor setup, student Intuit ID sign-in, and NGAME mirror testing · May 2026

Round-one lab model: Students post transactions in one shared QBO sandbox company (browser only). NGAME runs on one surveillance PC (read-only). No paid QBO licenses required for the POC.

Instructor checklist (before class)

  1. One sandbox realm_id in quickbooks_config.json (environment: sandbox).
  2. OAuth once on surveillance PC as Master or Company Admin.
  3. Test Manage users invite (your alt email) → post test invoice → one NGAME run.
  4. Bookmark sandbox QBO for students.
  5. Distribute student section (below) or this PDF.

For students — how you get credentials

You are not signing up as a developer. You need a free Intuit account (Intuit ID) when the instructor invites you to the lab company.

First time

  1. Open the invitation email from QuickBooks.
  2. Click the link (may expire ~48 hours — ask instructor to resend).
  3. New: Create account at accounts.intuit.com.
  4. Existing Intuit user: Sign in with that account.
  5. Accept access to the lab company.

Every class

  1. Go to sandbox.qbo.intuit.com (or instructor bookmark).
  2. Sign in with your Intuit ID.
  3. Select the lab company if asked.

You do not need: developer.intuit.com, NGAME install, or instructor passwords.

Instructor: invite a student

  1. Open lab sandbox in QBO → Settings → Manage users → Add user.
  2. School email + role Standard (typical) → Send invitation.

NGAME OAuth vs student UI

Only Company/Master Admin authorizes NGAME (you, once). Students with Standard role use the UI only — expected.

Mirror testing

Same sandbox company as NGAME → student edits appear on next training/fraud run. See NGAME_Churn_Walkthrough for CPI / wCPI / mutations.
